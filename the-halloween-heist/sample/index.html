The cobblestone streets of the kingdom were littered with golden leaves, and the air smelled faintly of cinnamon and adventure...

The sound of distant laughter echoed through the alleys, but Peregrine knew this was no ordinary Halloween. A mysterious thief had been spotted near the royal pumpkin patch, and the night’s magic felt… different.
